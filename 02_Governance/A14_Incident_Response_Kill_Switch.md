# A14 — Incident Response & Kill-Switch Governance

**Part of:** Controlled AI Core™ (CAIC)  
**Version:** 2.2  
**Status:** Normative  
**Year:** 2026  
**Author:** Albert Shamin  
**Trademark:** Controlled AI Core™

**PUBLIC SUMMARY** – This document is an abbreviated overview of the Controlled AI Core™ Standard. The full implementation specification is not publicly available.

---

## 1. Purpose

Defines an emergency stop mechanism (Kill‑Switch) that immediately halts AI operations, accessible only to authorised persons (typically the AISO).

## 2. Kill‑Switch Characteristics

Immediate, irreversible, auditable, and resilient – works even if the AI or control plane is compromised.

## 3. Incident Response

After activation, all interactions stop; a formal review must be completed before reactivation.

## 4. Testing

Regular drills are required to verify functionality.
