# A13 — AI System Owner (AISO) Model

**Part of:** Controlled AI Core™ (CAIC)  
**Version:** 2.2  
**Status:** Normative  
**Year:** 2026  
**Author:** Albert Shamin  
**Trademark:** Controlled AI Core™

**PUBLIC SUMMARY** – This document is an abbreviated overview of the Controlled AI Core™ Standard. The full implementation specification is not publicly available.

---

## 1. Purpose

Establishes the mandatory role of the AI System Owner (AISO) – a single accountable human.

## 2. AISO Responsibilities

Approves autonomy levels and policies, oversees operations, activates the Kill‑Switch if needed, signs the Compliance Statement, and acts as the primary contact for governance.

## 3. Appointment

Appointed by the organisation; accountability is non‑delegable.

## 4. Separation of Duties

Should not be the primary developer (to avoid conflict of interest).
